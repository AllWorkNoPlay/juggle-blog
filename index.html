Beginner's Guide to Juggling 3 Balls

Getting Started

Juggling 3 balls might seem challenging, but with practice, anyone can learn!

Basic Stance

  • Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
  • Keep your elbows close to your body
  • Look straight ahead, not at your hands

Practice Stages

  1. Single Ball: Toss one ball in an arc from one hand to the other
  2. Two Balls: Practice exchanging two balls between hands
  3. Three Ball Cascade: The classic juggling pattern

Tips for Success

Practice consistently, start slow, and don't get discouraged. Juggling is a skill that improves with time!
